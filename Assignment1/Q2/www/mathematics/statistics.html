Course Description

Introduction of statistics is a branch of mathematics that is exploring and dealing
with the collection data analysis, bias and the mean squared error of estimates;
simple ANOVA and hypothesis testing. Statistics is the presentation of
masses by numerical data through the introduction of experimental design
and the analysis of variance.
